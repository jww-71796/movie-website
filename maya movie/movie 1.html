The Pursuit of Happyness

2006 Drama • Biography 8.0/10 IMDb

Character I Admire: Chris Gardner

Played by: Will Smith

Occupation: Medical device salesman turned stockbroker

Chris Gardner is a true inspiration. Despite facing homelessness with his young son, he never gives up on his dream. His resilience, intelligence, and unwavering determination to provide a better life for his son make him one of the most admirable characters in cinema.

Movie Summary

The Pursuit of Happyness is based on the true story of Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman in San Francisco. After investing his life savings in portable bone density scanners, Chris faces financial ruin when the devices don't sell as expected.

His wife leaves him, he loses his apartment, and he's forced to live in shelters with his 5-year-old son Christopher. Despite these challenges, Chris pursues an unpaid internship at Dean Witter Reynolds, competing against 19 other candidates for one paid position. The film follows his journey through homelessness while trying to provide for his son and secure a better future.

Life Lessons & Personal Growth

1. Perseverance Against All Odds

Chris teaches us that success isn't about never falling down, but about getting up every time you fall. He faced rejection, homelessness, and despair but never stopped pursuing his goal.

2. The True Meaning of Happiness

The movie shows that happiness isn't about material wealth but about pursuing what gives your life meaning and purpose. Chris finds happiness in his relationship with his son and his pursuit of a better life.

3. Sacrifice for Loved Ones

Chris's willingness to endure immense hardship for his son's future taught me about parental love and sacrifice. He worked tirelessly, often going hungry so his son could eat.
